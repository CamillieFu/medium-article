How to Survive at the Bottom of the Ocean

Sometimes You Just Sink

There isn't always a silver lining. Sometimes there is nothing but rock bottom. Nevertheless, there are still reasons to persist, even in such hostile quarters.

Sometimes You Float a Bit

Oftentimes, after jumping persistenly for long enough, your feet may lift up off the ground. The force of the current, the force of gravity, the pull of the earth may force you back down but, you did it! You lifted off. If only for a brief moment. You proved to yourself. Things change. Things can change.

Sometime You Soar

The elusive glide which seems to ever evade our grasp can and does sometimes flitter down within our reach. Sometimes we soar. Fucking soar! Autonomously and powerfully pushing through the sky up and away from the ocean. Did it always look so beautiful? So blue and dark. It was always this terrifying and deep. It seems to beckon menacingly calling back down to that rocky bottom.
